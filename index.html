FAQ

Questions clients often ask before entering the NYC commercial market.

How should a company begin a Manhattan office space search?

Begin with use, headcount, client access, employee commute patterns, budget, lease term, and desired building identity. A disciplined search compares Midtown, FiDi, and other Manhattan submarkets before negotiating economics and flexibility.

What matters most when leasing Queens warehouse space?

Loading, ceiling height, column spacing, power, zoning, truck access, parking, proximity to highways, and lease structure are central. LIC, Maspeth, Jamaica, and nearby Queens markets each serve different industrial and logistics needs.

Is Chinatown retail suitable for restaurant space?

Chinatown can be strong for restaurant and specialty retail uses, but each storefront must be reviewed for venting, gas, frontage, foot traffic, layout, licensing considerations, and the strength of the surrounding tenant mix.

Do international buyers need a different NYC advisory process?

International buyers benefit from clear local market context, ownership structure guidance, income analysis, property management planning, and a long-term investment strategy that accounts for New York’s operating realities.
